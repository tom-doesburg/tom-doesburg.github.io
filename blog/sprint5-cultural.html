Sprint Five - Reflections

What are three new things you have learned about yourself and your ego as a result of the core learning?

I have learnt that I need to practice remaining focussed and I need to have good systems in place in order to work efficiently. I have learnt that when my inner voice is telling me that “I can’t do it”, I need to push on and there is a breakthrough just around the corner. I have learnt that when I am in my flow I can get a lot of work done. This helps my ego in terms of reminding me that I can do.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

I was surprised by how much studying about learning helped me understand the course material.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

I was surprised by how much I didn’t know about myself. I really had to think about my identity and values.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development bootcamp course?

It’s because it adds to making a more well rounded worker. From experience, you need to use all these skills in a work environment. It’s not just about having technical skills it’s about having human skills as well.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practicing programming instead? Justify your answer.

I do think that I need to improve my programming skills before bootcamp starts. However, I definitely don’t think the time spent on core learning was a waste. I am more efficient and I know how to deal with obstacles from having spent time studying core learning.
