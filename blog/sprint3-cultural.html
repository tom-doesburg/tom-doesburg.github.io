Sprint Three - Emotional Intelligence

** What is Emotional Intelligence? ** Emotional Intelligence or EQ, is the ability to know your own emotions and understand the emotions of others. This helps guide decision making and helps to really connect with people on an emotional level. Knowing what somebody is feeling understanding of what they are thinking.


** How is it different to IQ? ** IQ is about your logical reasoning, reading comprehension, mathematical and abstract thinking ability. Generally, IQ is based more on memory and the ability to learn, understand and apply information


** Why is it important? ** Emotional intelligence is important as it helps create strong connections with people. High EQ is important when working in teams and in leadership roles. As human beings, we all share the same emotions and being able to identify other peoples emotions means that you can adjust your own behaviour and adapt emotionally to what is in front of you.
